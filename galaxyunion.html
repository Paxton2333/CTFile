Galaxy Union

About Galaxy Union

Galaxy Union is a remarkable organization founded by Zi Tong in the year 2020. It stands as a testament to human ingenuity, determination, and the unending pursuit of knowledge and power.

At its core, Galaxy Union is driven by a singular goal: to unite with other forces, both terrestrial and cosmic, in the ceaseless quest for strength. It operates on the principle that collaboration and cooperation are the keys to unlocking the full potential of humanity.

One distinguishing aspect of Galaxy Union is its unique composition. The majority of its members and its formidable army are composed of Cultivators, individuals who have honed their minds and bodies to harness extraordinary abilities. These Cultivators come from diverse backgrounds, each with their own story of perseverance and self-discovery.

Galaxy Union is not merely an organization; it is a living testament to the boundless potential of human beings when they work together with a common purpose. The organization has become a symbol of hope and inspiration for those who yearn for a better future.

Over the years, Galaxy Union has formed alliances with various groups, fostering diplomatic ties and mutual support. Together, they explore the far reaches of the cosmos, seeking knowledge, resources, and allies that will aid in the pursuit of their shared goals.

As Galaxy Union continues to evolve and expand, it remains committed to the principles of unity, strength, and exploration. The journey ahead is fraught with challenges, but with individuals like Zi Tong at the helm and a legion of Cultivators by their side, there is no doubt that Galaxy Union will continue to shine brightly in the universe.
