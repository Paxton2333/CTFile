The B-Trios

About The B-Trios

The B-Trios is a clandestine group comprised of three individuals with extraordinary abilities. Its founding members, Bob, Bosh, and Brid, have been operating under the radar since around 2016, conducting a string of audacious heists that have left law enforcement agencies baffled.

Owner: Bob, Bosh, Brid

Since: Around 2016

Unique Abilities

The B-Trios is known for its members' distinct and unparalleled abilities:

  • Bob: Bob possesses superhuman strength, allowing him to effortlessly overpower obstacles and adversaries.
  • Bosh: Bosh has been gifted with extraordinary speed, enabling him to execute lightning-fast maneuvers and escape pursuers with ease.
  • Brid: Brid's remarkable power lies in teleportation, with a range of up to 1000 meters. This incredible ability allows Brid to appear and disappear at will, making capture nearly impossible.

Crime Spree

Since their emergence, The B-Trios has carried out a multitude of daring heists, totaling over a hundred. Their audacity knows no bounds, and their precise execution has repeatedly confounded law enforcement agencies. Even the most determined police forces have been unable to apprehend them.
