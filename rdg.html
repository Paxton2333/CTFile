Red Dust Group

About Red Dust Group

Red Dust Group, a name that resonates through the annals of supernatural history, was founded by a mysterious and enigmatic figure known as Nathan. While the exact date of its inception remains shrouded in the veils of time, historians and mystics estimate its formation to be somewhere between the late 1800s and early 1900s.

Nathan, the visionary behind this clandestine organization, possessed knowledge and abilities that transcended the comprehension of ordinary mortals. He embarked on a mission far beyond the scope of conventional understanding - the collection of souls that even the most seasoned Reapers dared not approach.

The primary objective of the Red Dust Group was the collection of souls, but not just any souls. They sought the spirits of beings whose existence straddled the line between the mortal realm and the ethereal. It was said that the Red Dust Group specialized in gathering the souls of malevolent entities and even the elusive spirits of maleficent deities.

Nathan's vision extended beyond the ordinary plane of existence, and his followers, carefully selected for their unique talents, embarked on perilous missions to retrieve these elusive souls. Armed with ancient knowledge and formidable powers, they ventured into the darkest corners of existence to fulfill their purpose.

The enigma surrounding the Red Dust Group's activities cast a shroud of fear and awe across the supernatural world. Whispers of their exploits echoed through the ages, with tales of epic battles against formidable adversaries and daring soul retrievals from the most treacherous realms.

While the true extent of their influence and their encounters with evil beings remains a subject of speculation, the Red Dust Group's legacy endures as a testament to Nathan's audacious quest to maintain balance between the realms. Their actions serve as a reminder that even in the realm of the supernatural, some mysteries are better left unsolved, and some souls are best left in the hands of those who understand the delicate art of the afterlife.
