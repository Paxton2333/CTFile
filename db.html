About Dong Heng and Bing Yi

Dong Heng

  • Name: Dong Heng
  • Height: 164 cm
  • Weight: 56 kg
  • Gender: Male
  • Appearance: A fox with a relatively large tail, overall yellow fur, fur on the ears and head, orange tip on the tail, white fur around the neck.
  • Personality: Gentle and kind
  • Traits:
    • Possesses a magical yellow soul with inherent lightning attributes since birth, can control and summon electricity.
    • Taught fireball magic by Bing Yi.
    • Gains temporary resistance to the effects of anesthesia, poison, and certain substances after exposure.
    • Not affected by cold and can transform into a small fox for unknown reasons.
  • Origin: Originally a regular human, but due to certain circumstances, became involved in a battle, sustained severe injuries, and his body was destroyed, leaving only his soul. His soul was then reconstructed into a new body by a mysterious organization, granting him the power of electricity. His body was created using materials that made him immune to a certain liquid, and he became a furry from that point onward.

Bing Yi

  • Name: Bing Yi
  • Height: 226 cm (182 cm in smaller form)
  • Weight: 88 kg (60 kg in smaller form)
  • Gender: Female
  • Appearance: A fox with an all-white body, elegant fur, and a tail almost as large as herself.
  • Personality: Gentle and kind
  • Traits:
    • Possesses a blue soul and learned both fire and ice magic from a magical book, combining them into ice-fire magic.
    • Carries a wristband with magical technology that can release an energy shield and produce an energy sword.
    • Bing Yi has two forms, with the smaller form acquired later. Her original height of 2.26 meters was when she was born.
    • Despite having her own body, she can merge with Dong Heng through a connection that allows her to teleport to his location or sense his experiences. Like Dong Heng, she is not affected by the cold.
  • Origin: Bing Yi is Dong Heng's tulpa, born when Dong Heng was severely injured. She was created by a spaceship organization, and her body was sent to Dong Heng using spaceship technology. She was absorbed into Dong Heng and gained her own body in the process.
