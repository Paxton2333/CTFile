Paxton's Abilities

Paxton’s Info:

Name: Paxton
Gender: Male
Age: 10 (estimated)
Paxton is the ancient, true god of the universe, endowed with an array of incredible abilities. He currently serves as the Captain and Owner of the Universe Guarding Base named C.T, wielding immense authority over all matters within the universe. Paxton, despite his immense powers, tends to be rather laid-back, but when the situation demands, he can focus his attention and abilities with unwavering determination.
