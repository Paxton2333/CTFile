The Antanist Family

A Legacy of Wealth and Power

The Antanist Family, a name that resonates with opulence and mystique, has spanned the annals of time. Established around the turn of the 19th century and enduring into the modern age, this family is a symbol of prosperity, vast riches, and an unwavering tradition of magic.

Guardians of Prosperity

Among their numerous distinctions, The Antanist Family possesses a lineage of powerful magicians who have safeguarded their legacy through generations. These magicians are not just keepers of secrets but also protectors of the family's immense wealth and property.

Heads of the Family

  • Endella Antanist (Age 18) - The eldest of the head members, Endella embodies the wisdom and leadership that has guided the family through the ages.
  • Anderella Antanist (Age 17) - The younger of the head members, often affectionately called "Andr" by Paxton, she carries the responsibility of shaping the family's future. Anderella has a particularly close bond with Paxton and is fond of him, even though Paxton sometimes get annoyed by her.
